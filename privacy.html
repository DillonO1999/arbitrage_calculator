Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At ArbitrageCalc, we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to register or provide personal information such as names or email addresses. However, we use third-party tools to help us understand how our site is used.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior. This service uses "cookies"—small text files placed on your device—to collect anonymous data such as your browser type, device, and the pages you visit. This helps us improve our tool for you.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

4. How to Opt-Out

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings or by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us via our GitHub repository profile.
