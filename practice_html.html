Understanding Arbitrage

What is it?

Arbitrage (or "Arbing") is a strategy where you place bets on all possible outcomes of an event across different sportsbooks to guarantee a profit regardless of the result.

The Math

To find an arb, you must convert American odds to Implied Probability. If the sum of probabilities is less than 100%, an arbitrage opportunity exists.

Step 1: Convert to Decimal Odds

Positive (+200): (200 / 100) + 1 = 3.00

Negative (-150): (100 / 150) + 1 = 1.66


Step 2: Calculate Implied Probability

P = (1 / Decimal Odds) × 100
